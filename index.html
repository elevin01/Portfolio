DOR - Digitized Observation Checklist

Role: Project Manager, Developer, Designer

Summary: Developed an iOS app that digitizes the observation checklist process, streamlining data collection, organization, and reporting for educational institutions. The project earned client approval and interest in future funding due to its potential to revolutionize data management in education.

  • Critical features implemented:

  • Created comprehensive technical documentation, including system specifications and architectural diagrams.
  • Designed the UI/UX using Figma, incorporating user feedback for iterative improvements.
  • Presented the project and demoed the application to the client, securing approval and alignment on future development plans.
